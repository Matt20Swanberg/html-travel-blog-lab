Adventures in Fire Island

My visit to Fire Island National Seashore was a peaceful escape from the city, defined by wide sandy beaches, rolling dunes, and quiet maritime forests.

The park's car-free communities create a calm, laid-back atmosphere where walking and biking are the primary ways to get around. I especially enjoyed climbing the historic Fire Island Lighthouse for panoramic ocean views and exploring the boardwalk trails that wind through protected dunes and wetlands. With its diverse wildlife, scenic shoreline, and natural beauty preserved along the Atlantic coast, Fire Island offers a refreshing blend of relaxation and outdoor adventure.
